About Me
From Ongole to Google

I was born and raised in Ongole, a coastal town in Andhra Pradesh, India. My academic journey took me through Sri Vivekananda Vidya Vihar, Narayana High School, and Sri Chaitanya Junior College before earning a B.Tech in Information Technology from QIS College of Engineering and Technology.

After launching my career as a Software Engineer at Sonata Software in Bangalore, I moved to the United States in 2021 to pursue my Master's in Computer Science at Governors State University in Illinois. Since then, I have built a career across multiple roles and organizations, eventually earning an H-1B visa and joining Google LLC through Synapse Design Automation Inc.

My journey has been shaped by persistence, adaptability, and a genuine desire to keep pushing forward. I have had the privilege of working with globally recognized organizations including NTT Data, Honeywell, Visa Inc., and Google.
